General Information

  • John Christopher Depp II was born in Owensboro, Kentucky on June 9th in 1963. As we all may know he is a very known actor; famous for being a producer, and musician. Can you believe that?

  • Johnny Depp; as people know him, is the youngest of four children of Betty Sue Palmer who was a waitress and John Christopher, who was a civil engineer. Throughout the years, he has grown into a very recognizable person who has impacted the world of film. With his humor and personality, he has definitely conquered the attention of millions of people, including adults as well as young children. He is an American actor that has been nominated for three Academy Award and has won the golden Globe and Screen Actors Guild Award.

  • Johnny Depp is also considered to be one of the worlds's biggest film stars, gaining alot of praise from his followers and reviewers. The amount of praise that he gains has made him be the third highest grossing actor; films featuring this actor have made over $10 billion dollars worldwide.
